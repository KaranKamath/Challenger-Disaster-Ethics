Case #2

Andrew Wakefield and the Vaccine–Autism Falsehood

  1. http://cnn.com
  2. http://nhs.uk

The Claim

A connection between autism and the combined MMR vaccine

The Consequences

  • Global Paranoia and the birth of the Anti-Vax Movement
  • Severe MMR outbreaks in the US and Europe
  • Widespread Suffering and Death

Key Ethical Transgressions

  • Wakefield didn't declare financial conflict of interest
  • Children subjected to tests not required or justified by study, constituting abuse
  • Link between 12 isolated cases and eventual "findings" fabricated, not actually grounded in medical records

Analysis–Duty Ethics

Conflict of Interest

  • Wakefield: Duty to conduct bias free research
  • Co-Authors: Were not vigilant during the research, but pulled support later
  • Media: Irresponsible journalism, violating their duty to report facts
  • Scientific Community: Initially accepted findings, did not fulfill duty to verify publication

Analysis–Duty Ethics

Child Medical Abuse

  • Wakefield: Failed to follow ethical protocol, violating his scientific duty
  • Co-Authors: Silence is acquiescence to Wakefield's methods
  • Media: Did not verify facts, violating journalistic duty

Analysis–Duty Ethics

Invalid Evidence

  • Wakefield: Duty to conduct bias free research
  • Co-Authors: Did not raise objections, violating their scientific duty
  • Media: Irresponsible journalism, violating their duty to report facts
  • Scientific Community: Violated scientific duty to verify publication evidence
